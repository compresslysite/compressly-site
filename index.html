Frequently Asked Questions

How many images can I compress at once?
Using the Bulk Compress tab, you can compress up to 20 images at once and download them all with one click.
How does this image compressor work?
Our tool uses the HTML5 Canvas API in your browser. Everything happens on your device — no data is ever sent to any server.
Will my images lose quality?
At 70–80% quality the difference is nearly invisible while file size drops dramatically.
What formats are supported?
We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats up to 20MB per image.
Is this tool really free?
Yes, 100% free. No account, no watermarks, no hidden charges.
Can I use this on my phone?
Absolutely. Compressly works perfectly on iOS and Android in any modern browser.
