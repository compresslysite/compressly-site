Frequently Asked Questions

How does this image compressor work?
Our tool uses the HTML5 Canvas API built into your browser to redraw and re-encode your image at a reduced quality level. This happens entirely on your device — no data is ever sent to any server.
Will my images lose quality?
Compression always involves some trade-off, but at 70–80% quality setting, the difference is nearly invisible to the human eye while file size drops dramatically. Use the preview to judge for yourself.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg), PNG (.png), and WebP (.webp) formats up to 20MB in size.
Is this tool really free?
Yes, 100% free. No account required, no watermarks added, no hidden charges. We keep the lights on through non-intrusive ads.
How much can I reduce my image file size?
Most images can be reduced by 50–90% depending on the original format, content, and your chosen quality level. A 3MB photo can easily become 300KB without visible quality loss.
Can I use this on my phone?
Absolutely. Compressly is fully responsive and works perfectly on iOS and Android devices in any modern browser.
