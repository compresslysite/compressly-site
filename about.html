Our Mission

Every day, millions of people need to compress images — for websites, social media, email, apps, and more. Most tools either charge money, require sign-ups, or upload your private images to unknown servers.

We built Compressly differently. Our tool runs entirely inside your browser. Your images never leave your device. No server. No storage. No risk.

We believe powerful tools should be accessible to everyone — designers, developers, students, bloggers, and everyday users around the world.
